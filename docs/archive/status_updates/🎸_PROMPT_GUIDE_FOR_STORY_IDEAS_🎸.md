## üí° TIPS FOR GETTING GREAT RESULTS

### **DO:**
‚úÖ **Use specific professions/character types**
   - Botanist, chef, musician, astronomer, photographer
   - Not: "person", "someone", "a character"

‚úÖ **Include ONE strong "what if" hook**
   - "What if songs could open doorways?"
   - "What if aging gave you superpowers?"
   - "What if you could taste emotions?"

‚úÖ **Add context if inspired by something**
   - "Inspired by 'Dark Star'"
   - "Drawing from 'Terrapin Station'"
   - "Themes from 'Ripple'"

‚úÖ **Use active, vivid verbs**
   - Discovers, realizes, encounters, uncovers
   - Not: "is", "has", "there is"

‚úÖ **Hint at conflict or cost**
   - "...but at a price"
   - "...only to discover"
   - "...must choose between"

‚úÖ **One sentence is enough!**
   - Let the AI expand the rest
   - Trust the system

### **DON'T:**
‚ùå **Over-specify details**
   - Don't name characters yet
   - Don't include full plot
   - Don't specify chapter structure

‚ùå **Be too vague**
   - Not: "A love story"
   - Not: "An adventure"
   - Not: "Something magical"

‚ùå **Use multiple concepts**
   - Pick ONE strong idea
   - Not: "A botanist who's also a time traveler and a detective"

‚ùå **Worry about perfection**
   - The AI will fill gaps
   - First idea is often best
   - You can refine later
