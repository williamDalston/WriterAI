IDEA:
In a world where dark secrets lurk beneath the surface, an unlikely hero must confront
their dark past and discovers the truth about everything they believed. Nothing is as
it seems, and when a shocking revelation threatens to destroy everything, they must
choose between love and duty in a race against time. Against all odds, this tale of
love and loss follows a journey of self-discovery as the protagonist faces their
greatest challenge and tries to uncover the mystery of the hidden truths that will
change everything forever. Nothing will ever be the same once the truth is revealed.

TITLE:
The Last Truth

GENRE:
Thriller

TONE:
Dark, suspenseful, emotionally charged

TARGET_LENGTH:
novella (30k)

SETTING:
A big city full of danger and secrets.

PROTAGONIST:
A determined young woman who works as an investigator. She's strong and independent.

ANTAGONIST:
A shadowy figure from her past who controls everything from the shadows.

CENTRAL_CONFLICT:
She must confront the dark secrets of her past and discover the truth before it's too late.

THEMES:
Truth, identity, betrayal, love
